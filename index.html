About

Algoz Interactive is a Startup that takes a fresh approach at In-Game Marketing Campaigns. Initiated in 2019, this project unites Students from the University of Bern and St. Gallen, Game Developers and Corporate Partners. Through the organization of seminars and the full support of marketing campaigns, Algoz interactive hopes to ensure the most pleasant and efficient advertising.

Static Content

With our experts, identify the best Games and Player Base to guarantee an optimal placement of your Advertisements. Analyze and find the most optimal point of contact with your desired Target Audience. Take advantage of marketing spots within the game client and/or leverage in-game clever placements, such as Billboards, Decorations, Markets to guarantee efficient yet non-intrusive campaigns. Static Placements are particularly well-suited for already well-known brands that what to refresh Brand Recognition.

Interactive Placement

Want your audience to interact more with your Brand? Enhance the Product Placement by engaging gamers in the process. Isolate various surprise factors, potential interactions to maximize the receptivity of your audience. Guarantee a feeling of surprise and excitement by soliciting engaging behaviours from the players. Interactive In-Game Advertizing is well-suited for lesser known Brands as it is a great way to create Brand Recognition.

Branded Microtransaction

Developing Brand Recognition is not enough? How about selling products and brand-related items within the game? Establish Partnerships with video game producers and take advantage of their online shops to push for personalized content. Develop specific high-quality Skins, Cosmetics and Events and integrate them within special offers in order to seize the opportunity to its full potential. Branded Micro-transactions are typically well-suited for Clothing and Food Companies.

Promo Content

Create a connexion between your existing Products and virtual Content. Develop In-Game Events, Special Campaigns or extensions with game producers to generate strong enthusiasm from your target audience or reward existing customer behavior within games through Vouchers and QR-Codes in their favorite Virtual Environment. Establish a long-lasting relation between your brand and audience. Promotional Content is well-suited for companies in need of a direct and highly impactful campaign.

E-Sports and Streams

The Gaming industry does not limit itself the game. Major games are known for their events or lively communities. Support the competitive scene and gather people around your brand in game-related events. Additionally, support Streamers and Video Creators to build up viewing content for the gaming audience. Help establish a Community surrounding the virtual experience, and gain notoriety among the fan base. E-sports and Streamers sponsoring well-suited for companies with product and services strongly related to the gaming industry.

Branded Games

Get in contact with our network of game developers and, alongside our regular consulting meetings, co-create a gaming experience connected to your marketing campaign. A long and exciting road to build up unforgettable emotions for enthusiast gamers. With our help, guide the gaming experience to match your desired target audience and ensure maximum compatibility with your own Branding Strategy. Hope on this exotic but potentially very rewarding solution and obtain your first Indie Game! Branded Games development is well-suited for very highly committed companies aiming to secure the highest and most long-lasting impact.
